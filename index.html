A LITTLE ABOUT US

We are an indie studio made up of dreamers and passionate video game enthusiasts. Our mission is to create fresh, immersive gameplay experiences with a unique personality. We aim to deliver titles that stand out through original mechanics, carefully crafted visuals, and a strong artistic identity.

We are currently developing our first video game: a rhythmic tower defense set inside a steampunk-style music box. In this adventure, you'll have to protect the ballerina —and with her, music itself— from mechanical notes that have come to life with the intent to destroy everything.

This is just the beginning of our journey, and we’re excited to share every step of the development with you. Follow us on social media to stay updated on news, progress, and exclusive behind-the-scenes content.
