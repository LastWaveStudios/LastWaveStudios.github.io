The game's story

In a universe where melodies come to life, you enter a music box that plays sweet notes in tune with an ethereal dance. This world of dreams and melodies is home to a ballet dancer, gracefully spinning on a platform, bringing the magic of music to life. However, in this harmonious place, a mechanical threat has emerged.

An army of robotic musical notes, made of metallic and resonant elements, marches toward the dancer, intending to interrupt her dance and end it. These creatures, once the soul of the melody, have twisted into a chaotic tangle of noise, following patterns that deviate from the original harmony.

Protect the ballerina and stop the mechanical notes from reaching her. You’ll use various towers in the form of musical instruments that respond to the rhythm of the music. Each tower activates with the melody from the music box, creating a unique defense system where every action depends on the beat. The towers include instruments like the piano, violin, drum, and trumpet.

Sharpen your ear and sense of rhythm as you journey to the heart of the music, where harmony must be restored, and the melody made eternal.

MEET OUR MECHANIC NOTES

Crotchet

Watch out for this rail-riding ground enemy. It may not be the fastest, but it steadily advances, smashing into your towers and the ballerina with brute force.

Minim

This ground enemy moves a bit slower than the crotchet, but don’t let its pace fool you — it packs a heavier punch. If it reaches your defenses, it will deal greater damage than you'd expect.

Crotchet rest

The only flying enemy in the game, this one glides along ceiling rails at a speed similar to the Shadows. It’s not very durable and deals less damage than others, but unlike the rest, it attacks from a distance — launching pieces at your towers and the ballerina.

Special Enemies

Semibreve

Moves slowly along the rails, but don’t let its pace fool you — its blows pack a serious punch. It slams your towers and the ballerina hard, and it’s tougher to take down than most enemies out there.

Quaver

Is lightning fast, striking twice in a flash. Each hit may seem weak, but don’t underestimate it — you need to destroy both notes to defeat it. Take down one, and the other will rage, speeding up and hitting even harder.

Game Mechanics

Phases

  • Preparation Phase: Place turrets in designated spots and assign their inputs.
  • Combat Phase: The enemy horde begins, and turrets must be activated according to the rhythm.

Turret Activation

Each turret follows the rhythm defined by its rhythmic pattern. This pattern changes with upgrades, modifying its behavior, such as altering its area of effect, range, damage, and/or attack frequency.

Rhythmic Patterns

Each turret has a fixed pattern based on its type and upgrades, lasting one measure, matching the background song’s beat. This ensures uniformity among turrets and allows for difficulty adjustments with regular rhythms or syncopations.

Difficulties

The game features 3 difficulty modes: “Piano,” “Mezzo-Forte,” and “Arpegio.” As difficulty increases, enemy stats multiply, increasing health, resistance, and damage depending on the enemy type:

  • Piano Mode: Base
  • Mezzo-Forte Mode: 1.25x multiplier
  • Arpegio Mode: 1.5x multiplier
  • Virtuoso Mode: 2x multiplier

Tactical Defenses

In addition to turrets and enemies, there are barriers, pushes, and slowdowns.

Economy

The in-game currency is Vinyls. Players start with a set amount of Vinyls to place their initial turrets and can earn more by defeating enemies to build or upgrade turrets. This creates a constant resource flow, encouraging efficient enemy elimination and optimal defense placement.

Towers

Here, instruments don’t just play — they fight. Each tower is a musical instrument with its own attack style, rhythm, and personality. Place them wisely, build harmonic combos, and protect the ballerina from the enemy’s relentless march.
From the heavy beats of percussion to the quick notes of wind instruments, each one plays a crucial role in this symphony of survival.

Background

You’ll find yourself inside an old music box, a place where the melody never stops and every corner pulses to the rhythm of its inner mechanism. In this enclosed, enchanted space, you’ll face various threats to protect what keeps the harmony of this world alive. Though it may seem fragile and delicate, its interior hides a constant tension between beauty and danger.
